# Session 02 — The Haunted House

**Date Played:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Players Present:** Snax, Toro, Short Stacks
**Location(s):** The Haunted House (above and below)
**DM Notes:**

## Summary

The party pushed deeper into the haunted house, discovered it was a smuggling front, and fought their way through a basement ambush and an alchemist's lair. They uncovered Sea Princes crest markings, a secret tunnel to the sea, and narrowly missed capturing a mysterious arcane user escaping by rowboat.

## Events

### Scene 1 — Upper Floors
The party found alchemical powders, footsteps leading to an upper room with a harbor view, and a bound man named Ned sporting a nasty head wound. Ned desperately tried to get them to leave.

### Scene 2 — Trap Door and Basement Ambush
The party discovered a hidden trap door. Below, Ned's associates sprung an ambush — which the party defeated.

### Scene 3 — The Smuggler's Room
A well-kept room revealed: a naval almanac (tides for 100 miles of coastline), Goblin command phrases ("stop," "come," "attack," etc.), a Bullseye Lantern, signal codes on parchment, and a spell book hidden in the desk.

**Signal Codes:**
- Long-short-short-short: "Is it safe?"
- Short-long-short-long: "Everything safe"
- Long-long-long: "Ready to unload. Come to ship"

### Scene 4 — The Alchemist
Ignoring obvious signs of skeletons behind a locked door, the party found the alchemist's lair and confronted the man himself — searching for a Philosopher's Stone. They recovered a bag of holding, lots of gold, and an unidentified magical stone. Ned turned on the party once he saw them winning.

### Scene 5 — Tunnels and Escape
A secret passage led into tunnels where the party surprised more smugglers bearing the crest of the Sea Princes. At the tunnel's end, an unnamed arcane user barely escaped onto a small rowboat heading out to sea.
