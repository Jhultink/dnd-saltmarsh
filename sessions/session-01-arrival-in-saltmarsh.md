# Session 01 — Arrival in Saltmarsh

**Date Played:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Players Present:** Snax, Toro, Short Stacks (Baja'eesmanon'abujascrunchee)
**Location(s):** The Sharkfin (ship), unnamed island, Saltmarsh (Wicker Goat, Winston's store, Empty Net)
**DM Notes:**

## Summary

The party met aboard the Sharkfin en route to Saltmarsh, but a #skillIssue during a storm caused the ship to wreck on a small island. After defeating two Sahuagin, they made it to Saltmarsh, navigated some tense local politics, and accepted a job from a young council member named Anders to investigate a supposedly haunted house.

## Events

### Scene 1 — The Sharkfin
The party met aboard the Sharkfin, captained by Grendanna (female half-orc, scarred face, graying red hair). First mate Rackham Byrne, a muscular gnome thug, rounded out the crew. While prepping for a storm, the four adventurers got acquainted.

### Scene 2 — Shipwreck Island
The ship crashed during the storm, stranding the party on a small coastal island alongside two Sahuagin — who they promptly defeated. Snax lost his shield in the chaos.

### Scene 3 — Saltmarsh First Impressions
At the Wicker Goat tavern, the party picked up local tension: 200 dwarves sent by the crown to mine the region had stirred resentment among some residents. Toro acquired a Staff of Fish Command (Trident of Fish Command per D&D Beyond) off a child. The party strong-armed Winston into returning Snax's shield, which he'd recovered from the wreck.

### Scene 4 — The Job
After a night at the rowdy Empty Net, the party heard a public address from Anders Solmor, a young city council member. He hired them to investigate a rumored haunted house: payment varied by PC (2 potions + 80gp for Short Stacks; 1 potion + 90gp for Snax; 100gp for Toro). Rumors: untold riches inside, former alchemist's property, no one has returned. The party journeyed out and found evidence of fresh tracks and heard creaking from the second floor.
