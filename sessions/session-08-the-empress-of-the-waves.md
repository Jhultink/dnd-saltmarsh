# Session 08 — The Empress of the Waves

**Date Played:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Players Present:** Snax, Toro, Short Stacks
**Location(s):** Saltmarsh (Anders' mansion), Azure Sea, The Empress of the Waves (derelict ship)
**DM Notes:**

## Summary

The party investigated Anders' home and found a ciphered letter, then sailed with the dwarven Stonehelm brothers to recover a locked chest from a derelict ship. They found disturbing notes about Lolth-worshipping druids, fought a half-orc cultist and his phase spiders, and were then attacked by a giant octopus.

## Events

### Scene 1 — Ciphered Letter
Before meeting Anders, the party snooped through his home and found a ciphered letter in his office. They couldn't read it — yet.

### Scene 2 — Anders' Job
Anders told them one of his ships, presumed lost, had been found. He offered 2000gp to recover a chest of promissory notes — with the explicit condition that the chest must be returned unopened.

### Scene 3 — The Stonehelm Brothers
The party met their escort at the docks:
- **Bramli Stonehelm** — older brother, pragmatic businessman
- **Skaldor Stonehelm** — younger brother, adventurous captain of their ship, the *Stonewind*

Both are from Granitepeak, a mountain region known for metalcraft. They left to seek adventure and trade, eventually settling in Saltmarsh as merchants.

### Scene 4 — Sahuagin Ambush at Sea
En route to the Empress, Sahuagin attacked the Stonewind. A hard fight — the party won.

### Scene 5 — Aboard the Empress of the Waves
The Stonehelms rowed the party over then returned to the Stonewind. The Empress showed clear signs of battle damage. The chest was found in the captain's quarters. The party also found four notes:

**The Storm** *(Captain's Log, Day 14)*: Ship survived a massive storm, anchored at an uncharted island.

**First Contact** *(deck, hastily scrawled)*: The island hid druids worshipping Lolth, the Queen of Spiders. Only a few crew survived the island tribes' attack.

**Capture and Consecration** *(pinned to the main mast, written in blood)*: The druids took the ship as Lolth's gift. Crew dead or scattered.

**Fracture of Power** *(hidden under a floorboard)*: Two Lolth-worshipping factions now war over the ship. Supplies aboard, but danger is imminent.

### Scene 6 — Krell and the Phase Spiders
In the hold, a half-orc named Krell sat at the center of a ceremonial circle. Devout follower of Lolth. He demanded a blood sacrifice. The party refused. Combat.

Krell and his two phase spiders were defeated — then a massive tentacle reached up the side of the ship through a porthole.
