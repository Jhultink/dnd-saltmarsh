**Date Played:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Players Present:** Snax, Short Stacks, Lok, Grenmiirr, Flemin
**Location(s):** Saltmarsh (streets, docks, waterfront)
**DM Notes:**

## Summary

The Battle for Saltmarsh. The party rallied allies, fought through Sahuagin and a Thessalahydra, witnessed Anders reveal himself as a Scarlet Brotherhood agent, saw Skerrin die, and discovered Winston is a silver dragon in disguise. The blue dragon escaped. Anders is in custody. The conspiracy is exposed — but the war is far from over.

### Scene 2 — The Battle Begins
Alongside lizardfolk warriors, Solomon, Rex, and Captain Hex's pirates, the party fought through the streets and waterfront. Enemies: Sahuagin in force, plus a Thessalahydra — all fought and defeated.

### Scene 3 — The Docks
At the docks: Locathah bodies everywhere — they had fallen defending the harbor. The Brightbeard brothers were fighting Sahuagin on the Stonewind. Then Anders appeared — and revealed himself. He thanked the party for retrieving the promissory notes and gestured toward the sea. A **blue dragon** roared in over Saltmarsh.

**Skerrin Wavechaser** entered the fight on Anders' side.

### Scene 4 — Winston's Secret
The battle began turning badly. Then a **silver dragon** dove into the fight — black infection spreading across one wing, but fighting hard and buying the party precious time. Over the course of the battle the silver dragon was knocked unconscious and reverted to its true form: **Winston**.

The halfling shopkeeper is a silver dragon.

### Scene 5 — Resolution
The party revived Winston, slew the remaining Sahuagin, and killed Skerrin (long overdue). Anders was taken into custody. The blue dragon escaped out over the Azure Sea.
