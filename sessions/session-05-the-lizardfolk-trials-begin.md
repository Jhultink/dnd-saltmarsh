# Session 05 — The Lizardfolk Trials Begin

**Date Played:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Players Present:** Snax, Toro, Short Stacks
**Location(s):** The Marsh, Lizardfolk Mound
**DM Notes:**

## Summary

The party battled Bullywugs in the marsh, won a Helm of Underwater Action, sighted the feared Black Dragon, reached the lizardfolk mound, were immediately caught trying to bluff their way in, and stood before the Lizardfolk Queen — who offered an alliance conditional on completing seven trials. The Trial of Strength was completed.

## Events

### Scene 1 — Bullywug Challenge
Entering the marsh, the party heard chanting: "Gulpa'Gor... Gulpa'Gor..." Bullywugs rose from the water, but through translation magic the party learned they wanted a non-lethal duel over an artifact they'd found during their wanderings. The party won and received the artifact.

### Scene 2 — Night in the Marsh
The party camped. A dark shape moved silently across the sky — almost certainly the feared Black Dragon of these marshes.

### Scene 3 — The Mound
After crossing a river, the party reached the mound matching the location on their hand-drawn map. Their "sneaky" entrance was immediately detected; five guards caught them.

### Scene 4 — Before the Queen
The party claimed to work with the Sea Princes and produced items from the Sea Ghost to back it up. The guards disarmed them and brought them to the Queen. The Queen's interrogation was going okay — until one of the lizardfolk from the Sea Ghost identified them. The lie collapsed.

The Queen explained: the weapons were to retake their homeland from the Sahuagin who seized it roughly a year ago. She is open to an alliance with Saltmarsh but the party must prove their worth through seven trials overseen by Saurix, an elderly lizardfolk who once lived in Saltmarsh.

**The Seven Trials:**
1. **Strength** — 1v1 hand-to-hand against a lizardfolk warrior
2. **Knowledge** — Riddles with Saurix
3. **Bravery** — Fight Thousand-Tooth, the giant crocodile
4. **Faith** — Task from the Lizardfolk Shamans
5. **Etiquette** — Task for the Locathah, lizardfolk allies
6. **Speed** — Obstacle course for the Merfolk
7. **Negotiation** — Recover lizardfolk eggs from Xindrad the Fierce, a black dragon who "protects" the mound at the cost of monthly egg tributes

### Scene 5 — Trial of Strength
Snacks stepped up and defeated the lizardfolk warrior in unarmed combat.
