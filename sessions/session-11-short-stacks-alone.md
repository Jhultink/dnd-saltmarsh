# Session 11 — Short Stacks Alone

**Date Played:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Players Present:** Short Stacks (solo session)
**Location(s):** Gellan Primewater's Mansion (basement and adjacent rooms)
**DM Notes:**

## Summary

A solo session. Short Stacks infiltrated the depths of Gellan's mansion, navigated traps, discovered weapon caches labeled for both lizardfolk and Sahuagin, found a magical sword, and stole damning notes from two mysterious figures without being detected.

## Events

### Scene 1 — Navigating the Basement
Short Stacks worked his way through the basement, avoiding traps. He found boxes of weapons — strikingly, labeled for delivery to both the lizardfolk and the Sahuagin. Someone is arming both sides. He also discovered a magical sword among the crates.

### Scene 2 — The Two Figures
Short Stacks crept into an adjacent room and found two people in quiet conversation: a hooded figure and a half-genasi fire woman. He slipped behind them unnoticed and lifted a bundle of notes.

**The stolen notes revealed:**
- Payment records: bribes to local dockworkers, guards, and minor council officials to prevent dock inspections
- An unsigned warning to Gellan about "the new faces in Saltmarsh" — advises tightening security on "special shipments"
- A partially burnt note referencing Gellan's willingness to sabotage competing merchants' ships

### Scene 3 — The Exit
Short Stacks slipped back out through the basement without being detected.
