# Session 15 — Confessions at the Docks

**Date Played:** 2026-05-07
**Players Present:** Snax, Short Stacks, Lok, Grenmiirr, Flemin
**Location(s):** Saltmarsh docks
**DM Notes:**

## Summary

A short session in the aftermath of the battle. The party interrogated a restrained Anders, who admitted he funneled the promissory notes into gold to pay the blue dragon. Old wounds were reopened when Eda and Hex were revealed to be siblings. Winston, recovering from the fight, reverted to his halfling form and came clean — he is a dragon, cursed by a necromancer, and quietly asked the party for their help in breaking that curse. With loose ends on the table, the party agreed to sail with Captain Hex to hunt down the Shadow.

## Events

### Scene 1 — Anders in Chains
The party questioned Anders at the docks, where he remained bound and under guard. He admitted that he had converted all of the promissory notes to gold and delivered the funds to the blue dragon — his patron. The notes are gone. The debt has been paid. Anders had no apparent remorse.

### Scene 2 — Eda and Hex
During the aftermath, the party learned that Eda and Captain Hex are siblings. The revelation recontextualized Hex's presence throughout the campaign and raised new questions about Eda's loyalties and history.

### Scene 3 — Winston's Confession
Winston, stabilized after the battle, shifted back into his halfling form. He sat with the party and told them the truth: he is a dragon — not the blue dragon that attacked, but a silver dragon — and he has been cursed by a necromancer. The black infection on his wing is the mark of that curse. He asked, quietly and carefully, for the party's help in lifting it.

### Scene 4 — Anders Taken to Prison
Anders was led away from the docks to Saltmarsh prison. With Skerrin dead, Gellan missing, and Eliander murdered, Eda is now one of the last council figure standing (along with Manistrad) and has taken effective control of the town.

### Scene 5 — Choosing a Course
With Anders imprisoned and Saltmarsh in Eda's hands, the party made their next move: sail with Captain Hex to track down the Shadow. The blood oath stands.
