# Session 06 — Trials: Knowledge, Bravery, and Faith

**Date Played:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Players Present:** Snax, Toro, Short Stacks
**Location(s):** Lizardfolk Mound
**DM Notes:**

## Summary

The party completed three more of the seven lizardfolk trials — Knowledge, Bravery, and Faith — and gathered intel on Xindrad the Fierce ahead of the Negotiation trial.

## Events

### Scene 1 — Trial of Knowledge
Saurix led the party to a cave where an illusion made the floor appear to crumble away beneath them. He posed five riddles; the party answered three correctly. A passing grade.

### Scene 2 — Trial of Bravery
The party fought Thousand-Tooth, the enormous crocodile that had claimed one of the mound's freshwater sources. They slew it and reclaimed the water for the lizardfolk.

### Scene 3 — Trial of Faith
The lizardfolk shamans tasked the party with wrangling three giant lizards into cages — without harming the animals. The shamans were clearly hoping the party would fail or hurt the lizards, but despite their interference the party managed to cage all three. Barely.

### Scene 4 — Intel on Xindrad
After the trials, the party interviewed a lizardfolk soldier who shared that Xindrad the Fierce has been increasingly demanding: beyond eggs, he has begun asking for magical artifacts and gold as tribute.
