# Session 10 — Death of a Dragon

**Date Played:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Players Present:** Snax, Toro, Short Stacks
**Location(s):** Xindrad's Lair (the marsh), Lizardfolk Mound, Saltmarsh
**DM Notes:**

## Summary

The party killed Xindrad the Fierce, but lost Toro in the fight. They returned to Saltmarsh with 20 lizardfolk warriors, cleared their names with the dragon's head, and began piecing together the Scarlet Brotherhood conspiracy.

## Events

### Scene 1 — Killing Xindrad
The party talked Xindrad out of his lair, then dropped a Web spell on him — heavy damage dealt immediately. Fungi creatures emerged from the acid pool and periodically incapacitated party members. Xindrad focused on Toro, who went down. Short Stacks also fell. Snacks killed Xindrad and stabilized Short Stacks, but Toro had already died and could not be revived.

### Scene 2 — The Lizardfolk Mound
The party brought Toro's body into the mound. The Lizardfolk Queen honored their victory and sent them back to Saltmarsh with 20 armed lizardfolk warriors.

### Scene 3 — Back in Saltmarsh
Presenting Xindrad's severed head quieted any remaining suspicion about the well poisoning or storehouse arson. Eda hinted at a future task but wasn't ready to share details.

### Scene 4 — Land and Burial
Boris sold the party a cottage on ~3 acres of land north of Saltmarsh, overlooking the ocean. The 20 lizardfolk camped on the property. Toro was buried in a gravesite on the bluff overlooking the sea.

### Scene 5 — Winston's Revelation
Winston provided a partial translation of the ciphered letter recovered from Anders' house:

> *"...in exchange, we shall ensure that the Scarlet Brotherhood gains control of Saltmarsh... This effort is crucial to our mutual interests. Act quickly and discreetly... in secrecy and strength..."*

Winston also introduced the party to Lok, a dwarven friend of his, who agreed to work with them.

### Scene 6 — Gellan's Estate
The party attempted to infiltrate Gellan Primewater's estate. Gellan threw them out immediately — but Short Stacks slipped through the kitchen and into the basement before the door closed.
