Session 12 — Assassins at the Cottage

Date Played: YYYY-MM-DD Players Present: Snax, Toro (deceased), Short Stacks, Lok Location(s): Party Cottage (north of Saltmarsh), Saltmarsh, Shipwreck Pass, Azure Sea DM Notes:

Summary​

Three Sea Princes assassins attacked the party's cottage at night — including Nunya (Sanbalet) himself. The party defeated them all. In the morning they learned of a Sahuagin night raid and Gellan's disappearance. Eda sent them to Black Skull Cove to meet a pirate contact. Navigating Shipwreck Pass, they fought a water elemental and Sea Hags before reaching the cove.

Events​

Scene 1 — Reunion at the Cottage​

After being ejected from Gellan's mansion, the three players reunited at the seaside cottage. Late-night conversation over sausages. Lok and Short Stacks went to bed; Snacks stayed up by Toro's grave.

Scene 2 — The Assassins​

Three figures infiltrated the cottage: Nunya (Sanbalet), the hooded man Short Stacks had seen in Gellan's basement, and the half-genasi fire woman. All three bore Sea Princes tattoos. The party defeated all three.

Scene 3 — Morning News in Saltmarsh​

  • A Sahuagin scouting party attacked the town during the night.
  • Gellan Primewater has vanished.

Scene 4 — Eda's Mission​

Eda pulled the party aside. She needs them to travel to Black Skull Cove and deliver a sealed letter to a pirate she knows. She instructed them not to open it. In exchange for the intel, she took control of Gellan's weapon stash to arm the town's defenses. She also gave them a glass orb — when thrown into water, it expands into a medium-sized sloop.

Scene 5 — Shipwreck Pass​

The party launched the Sloop and navigated Shipwreck Pass, a narrow channel through sinister coastal bluffs.

  • A water elemental blocked their path. The party appeased it by offering magical items and it let them through.
  • Sea Hags attacked alongside a summoned Merrow. A hard fight — the party narrowly won.
