# Session 04 — Sinking the Sea Ghost

**Date Played:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Players Present:** Snax, Toro, Short Stacks
**Location(s):** Sea Ghost (Azure Sea), Saltmarsh (Anders' mansion, Council chambers, Winston's, Xendros')
**DM Notes:**

## Summary

The party won the fight aboard the Sea Ghost, freed a sea elf prisoner named Oceanus, and sank the ship. Back in Saltmarsh, the council rewarded them and tasked them with investigating the lizardfolk — peacefully.

## Events

### Scene 1 — Victory on the Sea Ghost
The party won a narrow fight, taking a young sailor named Charlie captive. Charlie couldn't reveal much about the Sea Princes' larger goals. Three lizardfolk escaped through a porthole — armed but not aggressive toward the party. The party sank the ship after looting it.

### Scene 2 — Discoveries Aboard
- ~1000gp worth of electrum and fine metals (split between captain's and lizardfolk quarters)
- Smuggled weapons, one bearing a forked lizard tongue crest
- Several maps, including a hand-drawn one marking a location to the West
- Oceanus: a tall sea elf found locked in a hidden prison cell at the ship's stern

**Oceanus' account:** He is from the tribe Manaan (undersea settlement ~20 miles SE of Saltmarsh). His chief sent him to surveil the Sea Ghost. He witnessed weapons being loaded at its home port, was discovered, and has been a prisoner since.

### Scene 3 — Back in Saltmarsh (4am)
The party arrived at Anders' mansion. In the dark, a shape moved nearby — unacknowledged. Skerrin and Anders spoke with them; Anders agreed to call a council meeting in the morning.

### Scene 4 — The Council Votes Again
The council was pleased the Sea Ghost was sunk but alarmed by the lizardfolk weapons connection. Two camps emerged: Manistrad and Anders wanted an aggressive approach; Gellan, Eda, and Eliander preferred a peaceful investigation. The peaceful camp won. The party was offered 700gp total to investigate — Oceanus hired on for 100gp of that as a guide.

### Scene 5 — Preparing for the Marsh
- Eliander pointed them to Winston, who had recently seen lizardfolk.
- Winston: 30+ lizardfolk entered a mound ~10 miles west. A large Black Dragon lairs in those marshes — be warned.
- Ordered 10 healing potion kits from Winston (100gp down, 100gp on delivery).
- Bought a Wand of Tongues from Xendros (3 charges, regains 1d4 at dawn; casts Tongues).
- Sharky sent to find a recipe for better healing potions.
- Took a long rest, then headed west into the marsh.
