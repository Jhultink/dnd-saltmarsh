# Session 09 — Conspiracy and Crisis

**Date Played:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Players Present:** Snax, Toro, Short Stacks
**Location(s):** Azure Sea (Stonewind), Saltmarsh (Anders' mansion, Xendros', Winston's, Anders' house again, Wicker Goat)
**DM Notes:**

## Summary

The party escaped the capsizing Empress via Toro's Rope Trick, collected their 2000gp reward from Anders, befriended Winston, stole the cipher key from Skerrin's room, and established an alibi while Winston decoded the letter — only to wake up to reports of arson and a poisoned well, with eyewitnesses pointing at them.

## Events

### Scene 1 — Escape from the Empress
Toro used Rope Trick to get the party out of the capsizing ship. The Stonehelm brothers picked them up. En route back, the brothers voiced suspicion that Gellan Primewater might be a smuggler.

### Scene 2 — Paying Out
Anders honored the deal and opened the chest in the party's presence. Inside: stacks of papers and documents. Whatever he needed was in there — the significance of the contents wasn't immediately clear.

### Scene 3 — Xendros
Xendros appeared through a magic portal from what looked like a fire realm. She noted she has been keeping an eye on the party's progress.

### Scene 4 — Winston Befriended
The party finally won Winston over. He opened up: he has left his adventuring life behind and made a vague, uncomfortable reference to a necromancer from his past — someone who drove him to hide in Saltmarsh.

### Scene 5 — Skerrin's Room
The party snuck back into Anders' house and found the cipher key in Skerrin's bedroom. Skerrin spotted them on their way out. They fled.

### Scene 6 — The Alibi
The party took the letter and key to Winston, who agreed to decode it and handed them invisibility potions. Invisible, they went to a bar and made sure to be "seen" there all night before heading to the Wicker Goat.

### Scene 7 — Morning News
Solomon and Eda returned; everything the party had claimed was confirmed true. A scouting force is being dispatched to look at the Sahuagin fortress. Guards posted throughout the city.

Eda confronted the party about their deal with Xindrad — the party cannot deliver Sahuagin servants, and Xindrad must be dealt with.

The party spent a day crafting healing potions. The next morning: the town well had been poisoned and the storehouse burned down. Arson. Witnesses described a group matching the party's description as the culprits.
