Session 07 — Trials Complete, Trouble at Home

Date Played: YYYY-MM-DD Players Present: Snax, Toro, Short Stacks Location(s): Lizardfolk Mound, Xindrad's Lair, Saltmarsh DM Notes:

Summary​

The party completed the final three lizardfolk trials — impressing the Locathah, barely surviving the Merfolk obstacle course, and negotiating a dangerously tentative truce with the black dragon Xindrad. They returned to Saltmarsh to find chaos: Eliander murdered, Skerrin suspiciously elevated to council, and dark rumors about Anders and Skerrin.

Events​

Scene 1 — Trial of Etiquette (Locathah)​

Through gifts, artistic expression, and cooking, the party won over the Locathah quickly. In return, the Locathah gave them seaweed that extends how long the consumer can hold their breath.

Scene 2 — Trial of Speed (Merfolk)​

The party ran a Merfolk obstacle course and performed... poorly. The Mermen and Merwomen were unimpressed. They passed, but barely.

Scene 3 — Trial of Negotiation (Xindrad the Fierce)​

The party tried to sneak up on Xindrad at his lair outside the mound. He was already waiting. Xindrad is a dragon — he knew they were coming. The party offered 200gp in electrum as payment for the remaining eggs, claiming that was everything they had. Xindrad saw through the lie and ate one of the 18 remaining eggs to make his point. Only Snacks' proposal — future Sahuagin servants for Xindrad, once the Sahuagin are defeated — kept the dragon from eating the party. A very tentative alliance formed.

Scene 4 — Return to Saltmarsh​

The Queen sent the party back with a sealed letter for the council. On the outskirts of Saltmarsh, they found Solomon drilling soldiers. He explained that Oceanus returned earlier claiming the party was dead, and meanwhile Eliander Fireborn had been murdered — a lizardfolk sword found by his body.

Scene 5 — Political Fallout​

Eda plans to lead a diplomatic delegation to the lizardfolk the next morning. Skerrin Wavechaser, Anders' butler, has been appointed to replace Eliander on the council. Manistrad pulled the party aside: she doesn't trust Skerrin or Anders, though she can't say exactly why.

The party investigated Eliander's burnt-out home. Not much remained — but the back door lock had been picked.

Scene 6 — Downtime and Shopping​

  • Sharky returned with a Greater Healing Potion recipe: 4d4+2 healing, 100gp to craft (throwable version for 20gp more, heals all in 5ft radius).
  • Sharky is pursuing a tavern apprenticeship.
  • Visited Xendros; shopped for magic items. Requests logged: Snacks wanted AC/movement boosts; Toro wanted curse removal for the Luck Stone.
  • Paid Winston the remaining 100gp for healing supplies. Negotiated a bulk deal: 200gp upfront + 200gp on delivery for 500gp worth of supplies.
  • Winston let slip he had a run-in with Xindrad about 15 years ago in his adventuring days.
