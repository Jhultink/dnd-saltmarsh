# Session 03 — The Sea Ghost

**Date Played:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Players Present:** Snax, Toro, Short Stacks
**Location(s):** Saltmarsh (Snapping Line, Winston's, Anders' mansion, Council chambers, Xendros' shop, Haunted House), Azure Sea
**DM Notes:**

## Summary

After licking their wounds, the party debriefed the council, ran personal errands around town, met Xendros the tiefling merchant, and used the signal codes they'd found to lure the smuggler ship Sea Ghost — ending the session in a brawl on its deck.

## Events

### Scene 1 — Back in Town
"Nunya" escaped. The party returned to Saltmarsh with their haul and took a long rest. At the Snapping Line, a tavern owner tipped them to visit Xendros, a tiefling in town. They settled debts with Winston (casks of brandy), though he remained suspicious of their connection to smugglers.

### Scene 2 — Meeting Anders and the Council
The party accompanied Anders Solmor to his mansion, where they met his butler Skerrin Wavechaser. Anders asked them to appear before the town council in three days.

**Personal downtime:**
- Toro: Identified the stone as a Luck Stone; researched the god Iuz at the library.
- Short Stacks: Bought studded leather armor; made contact with Solomon, garrison captain, while carousing at the Wicker Goat.
- Snacks: Crafted healing potions; found an alternate recipe for 20gp each.

### Scene 3 — The Town Council
The party appeared before the full council:

| Member | Faction | Vote | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|---|
| Eda Oweland | Traditionalist | End smuggling | Wants safety, fears follow-up attack |
| Gellan Primewater | Traditionalist | Do nothing | Doesn't want to risk lives |
| Eliander Fireborn | Loyalist | End smuggling | Law and order (peg leg from owlbear) |
| Manistrad Copperlock | Loyalist | End smuggling | Protect dwarves and town |
| Anders Solmor | Traditionalist | Do nothing | Swayed by Skerrin |

3:2 vote — party offered 200gp to end the smuggling by any means they choose.

### Scene 4 — Xendros
Visited the red tiefling Xendros in her dark, red-lit shop. She offered magical items for purchase and said if they return in a week she may have more. She also offered to search for any specific magical item for 50gp.

### Scene 5 — Luring the Sea Ghost
Using the Bullseye Lantern and the signal codes, the party set up at the haunted house and waited. Per the tide almanac, the ship was expected in two nights. They camped, the ship arrived, they exchanged signals — then rowed out on a dinghy. When the Sea Ghost's captain refused to unload without payment, a fight broke out on deck.
