# Session 13 — Captain Hex and the Otyugh

**Date Played:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Players Present:** Snax, Short Stacks, Lok, Grenmiirr, Flemin
**Location(s):** Black Skull Keep (Cove), The Gullet (pirate market), Saltmarsh, Dwarven Mines
**DM Notes:**

## Summary

The party met Captain Hex — Eda's exiled brother — at Black Skull Keep and swore a blood oath to help him claim the Shadow in exchange for his help defending Saltmarsh. Back in Saltmarsh, Manistrad revealed a monster had been terrorizing the dwarven mines. The party cleared the mines, defeating an otyugh.

## Events

### Scene 1 — Opening Eda's Letter
Before meeting the pirate, the party opened the sealed letter. It read:

> *"My dear Hector, I hope this letter finds you well... The situation in Saltmarsh has become increasingly dangerous. I am sure the rumors of the Sahuagin have not escaped your ears... I believe your presence, and that of your crew, could turn the tide... With the right convincing, they may even be able to help you get the Shadow... If you decide to come back, I will do everything in my power to ensure your safety and clear your name. With all my love, Eda Oweland."*

### Scene 2 — Captain Hex
Captain Hex (real name Hector) is Eda's brother — living in exile at Black Skull Keep. He agreed to return to Saltmarsh and bring his crew to help with the battle, but only under one condition: the party must swear a blood oath to help him claim the **Shadow**, a phantom ship with no crew and no captain, surrounded by permanent storms and moving islands. The party pieced together that the *Empress of the Waves* had likely encountered the Shadow.

### Scene 3 — The Gullet
Some shopping in the Gullet, the pirate market at Black Skull Cove, before heading back.

### Scene 4 — Return to Saltmarsh
Hex docked the Stormcaller at the old haunted house (now abandoned). The party took Sloop into Saltmarsh proper. Eda and Manistrad met them, along with two of Manistrad's top fighters: Grenmiirr and Flemin.

Eda asked for help setting up defenses. Manistrad had a separate ask: a creature had been secretly attacking dwarves in the mines for months. The dwarves had kept it quiet to avoid panic. With the battle coming, they needed the mines cleared so the dwarves could fight.

### Scene 5 — The Mines
The party worked through the tunnels. At the end they found and fought an otyugh — a massive, foul aberration. Tough fight. They killed it.
