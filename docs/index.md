## About Saltmarsh

Saltmarsh is a fishing town with a complicated relationship with smugglers, sea elves, and the lizardfolk of the nearby marshes. What begins as a simple investigation of a haunted house quickly pulls the party into a web of conspiracy, sea monsters, and political intrigue.
