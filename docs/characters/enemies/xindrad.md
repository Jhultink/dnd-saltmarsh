# Xindrad the Fierce

- **Role:** Black dragon; self-appointed "protector" of the lizardfolk mound
- **Race:** Black Dragon
- **Affiliation:** None — independent predator
- **Status:** Deceased — killed by the party, led by Snax (Session 10)

## Appearance

A black dragon of notable size. Lairs outside the lizardfolk mound in a cavern with an acid pool, from which fungi creatures emerged during the battle.

## Personality & Motivation

Arrogant and predatory. Knew the party was coming when they tried to sneak up on him. Demanded monthly egg tributes from the lizardfolk, then escalated to gold and magical artifacts. Drew the line at having his lie called out — ate one of 18 remaining eggs to demonstrate his seriousness when the party tried to lowball him with 200gp in electrum.

Only the promise of Sahuagin servants stayed his hand. He found the offer genuinely interesting — a dragon who appreciated having enemies delivered to him.

## Methods & Resources

- Acid breath and direct combat capability.
- Fungi creatures in his lair's acid pool that periodically incapacitated combatants during the fight.
- Psychologically dominant — his mere known presence in the marsh kept the lizardfolk paying tribute for an extended period.

## Relationship to the Party

The party negotiated with him for Trial of Negotiation (Session 07) — a tense standoff where Snax's Sahuagin-servant proposal barely prevented the dragon from eating everyone. They returned in Session 10 with a Web spell ambush, dealt heavy damage immediately, and Snax finished him off.

## Story Notes

- Winston had a confrontation with Xindrad approximately 15 years before the campaign, during Winston's adventuring days.
- The Sahuagin-servant deal the party made to avoid being eaten is now void. It was always impossible to honor — Eda pushed the party to deal with Xindrad precisely because they couldn't deliver on it.
- His horde loot was taken by the party (Session 10; specific contents not detailed in session notes).
- His death freed the lizardfolk mound from tribute payments and was the act that secured the full lizardfolk alliance.
