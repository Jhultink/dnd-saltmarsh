# Ned

- **Role:** Haunted house lookout; Sea Princes informant
- **Race:** 
- **Affiliation:** Sea Princes smuggling operation
- **Status:** Fate unknown after Session 02

## Appearance

Found bound with a nasty head wound in the haunted house upper floor.

## Personality & Demeanor

Desperate and deceptive — bound and wounded, he desperately tried to get the party to leave rather than investigate further. When he saw the party gaining the upper hand against his associates in the alchemist's lair, he turned on them and attacked.

## Relationship to the Party

Encountered in Session 02. Initially appeared to be a victim — bound and injured. Was actually a lookout for the smuggling operation. Betrayed the party mid-combat. His fate after Session 02 was not recorded.

## Story Notes

- His role as "lookout" and the fact that he was found bound suggests he may have been restrained by his own associates as a precaution, or as punishment for some failure.
- Attacked the party when he saw an opportunity — made his allegiances clear.
