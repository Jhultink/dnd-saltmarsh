# Grendanna

- **Role:** Ship captain; captain of the Sharkfin
- **Race:** Half-Orc
- **Affiliation:** The Sharkfin (merchant/passenger vessel)
- **Location:** Azure Sea region

## Appearance

Female half-orc with a scarred face and graying red hair.

## Personality & Demeanor

Unknown beyond her role as captain.

## Relationship to the Party

The party met aboard her ship, the Sharkfin, en route to Saltmarsh. The ship wrecked in a storm. Her current status and the fate of the Sharkfin after the wreck are not detailed.

## Story Notes

- The Sharkfin wrecked on a small coastal island during a storm in Session 01. Two Sahuagin were also stranded on the island and were defeated by the party.
- It's unclear whether Grendanna survived the wreck or what happened to her afterward.
