Captain Hex

  • Role: Pirate captain; exile; Eda Oweland's brother
  • Real Name: Hector Oweland
  • Race:
  • Affiliation: His own crew; Black Skull Keep
  • Location: Black Skull Keep (Black Skull Cove) — now returned to Saltmarsh

Appearance​

Description unknown. Lives in a keep at Black Skull Cove, leading a pirate crew in exile.

Personality & Demeanor​

Pragmatic and deal-driven. Agreed to return to Saltmarsh and bring his crew to defend it, but only after extracting a blood oath in return. Deeply motivated by the Shadow — the phantom ship has clearly become an obsession or a purpose.

Relationship to the Party​

The party was sent to him by Eda (his sister) with a sealed letter. They opened it beforehand. He agreed to Eda's request — but made the party swear a blood oath to help him claim the Shadow in exchange for his military help. He docked his ship at the old haunted house and joined the Battle of Saltmarsh.

Useful To The Party​

  • Commands a pirate crew — a significant fighting force.
  • Participated in the Battle of Saltmarsh (Session 14).
  • Has knowledge of the Azure Sea and its secrets, presumably including the Shadow.

Story Notes​

  • Hex has been living in exile at Black Skull Cove for an unspecified reason. Eda's letter promises to clear his name if he returns.
  • The Shadow is a phantom ship: no crew, no captain, surrounded by permanent storms and moving islands. The party pieced together that the Empress of the Waves likely had a prior encounter with it.
  • The blood oath to claim the Shadow is now the active arc — the party agreed in Session 15 to sail with Hex and hunt it down.
  • Hex may have existing knowledge of the Shadow that he hasn't shared yet.
