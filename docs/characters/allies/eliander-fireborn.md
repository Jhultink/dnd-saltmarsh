# Eliander Fireborn

- **Role:** Town Councilmember (Loyalist faction); law enforcement advocate
- **Race:** 
- **Affiliation:** Saltmarsh Town Council; Loyalist faction
- **Location:** Deceased — murdered between Sessions 06 and 07
- **Status:** Deceased

## Appearance

Had a peg leg, reportedly from an owlbear encounter in his past.

## Personality & Demeanor

Law-and-order minded. Voted to end the smuggling operation on principle. Directed the party to Winston when they needed information about lizardfolk activity in the marsh.

## Relationship to the Party

One of the council members who helped direct the party's work. Was not personally close to them, but was a reliable institutional ally.

## Story Notes

- Murdered between Sessions 06 and 07. A lizardfolk sword was planted at the scene — framing the lizardfolk and nearly derailing the alliance.
- The real killer is almost certainly Skerrin Wavechaser, acting on behalf of the Scarlet Brotherhood or the Sea Princes.
- His home was burned. The party found the back door lock had been picked — confirming it was not an accident or a lizardfolk attack.
- Skerrin was appointed to replace him on the council — the speed of which Manistrad found suspicious.
- Skerrin is now dead (Session 14) but never confessed. Anders may know whether Skerrin killed Eliander.
