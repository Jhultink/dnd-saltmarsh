# Eda Oweland

- **Role:** Town Councilmember (Traditionalist faction)
- **Race:** 
- **Affiliation:** Saltmarsh Town Council
- **Location:** Saltmarsh

## Appearance

Description unknown.

## Personality & Demeanor

Pragmatic and cautious. Voted to end the smuggling operation to protect Saltmarsh from follow-up attacks, but also voted for a peaceful approach to the lizardfolk rather than aggression. Increasingly becomes one of the party's primary government contacts as the conspiracy deepens.

## Relationship to the Party

Has developed into the party's most reliable council ally. Confronted them directly about the impossible Xindrad deal (they promised Sahuagin servants to the dragon — she knew it couldn't be honored and pushed them to resolve it). Sent them to Black Skull Cove to recruit her exiled brother. Gave them the glass orb that transforms into a sloop.

## Useful To The Party

- Direct line to council decisions and town defenses.
- Intelligence on threats (confirmed the party's findings about the Sahuagin and Skerrin).
- Brokered the mission to Captain Hex with a personal letter.
- Took control of Gellan's confiscated weapon stash to arm town defenses.

## Story Notes

- Eda is Captain Hex's sister (real name Hector Oweland). She wrote him a personal letter asking him to return to Saltmarsh, using the party as couriers.
- She has been working to clear Hex's name and ensure his safe return.
- As of Session 15, she is effectively in control of Saltmarsh — Anders is imprisoned, Skerrin is dead, Gellan has vanished, and Eliander Fireborn was murdered. She is the last council figure standing other than Manistrad.
- The extent of her knowledge of the Scarlet Brotherhood conspiracy is unclear.
