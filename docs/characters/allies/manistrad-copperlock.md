# Manistrad Copperlock

- **Role:** Town Councilmember (Loyalist faction); representative of the dwarven mining operation
- **Race:** Dwarf
- **Affiliation:** Saltmarsh Town Council; Loyalist faction
- **Location:** Saltmarsh

## Appearance

Description unknown.

## Personality & Demeanor

Straightforward and protective of her people. Voted to end the smuggling operation and initially favored an aggressive approach to the lizardfolk — concerned about their weapons connection. Despite being in a different political camp than Eda, she reaches the same practical conclusions. Keeps her own counsel on matters she can't prove.

## Relationship to the Party

Pulled the party aside after Skerrin's appointment to express suspicion of both Skerrin and Anders — she couldn't say exactly why, but her instincts proved correct. Also brought the party in on the otyugh problem in the dwarven mines in Session 13, having kept it quiet for months to avoid panic.

## Useful To The Party

- Representative of the 200 dwarves the crown sent to mine the region — a significant military asset.
- Provided Grenmiirr and Flemin as fighting companions for the Battle of Saltmarsh.
- Insider knowledge of tensions around the dwarven mining presence.

## Story Notes

- The dwarven mining presence has stirred resentment among some Saltmarsh residents (Traditionalists especially).
- Manistrad's early suspicion of Skerrin — before any proof existed — is worth noting; she may have additional information she hasn't shared.
