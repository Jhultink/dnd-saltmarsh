# Winston

- **Role:** Shopkeeper; retired adventurer; secret silver dragon
- **Race:** Silver Dragon (polymorphed as a halfling)
- **Affiliation:** Saltmarsh (civilian)
- **Location:** His shop, Saltmarsh

## Appearance

In halfling form: a shopkeeper who presents as an ordinary, slightly suspicious small merchant. True form: a silver dragon — large, with a black infection spreading across one wing (as of Session 14), suggesting an ongoing injury or corruption.

## Personality & Demeanor

Initially suspicious and standoffish — slow to trust. Once won over, becomes a genuine and capable ally. Carries heavy secrets: a retired adventuring life, and a vague, troubled history involving a necromancer that drove him to hide in Saltmarsh. Agreed to decode the ciphered letter for the party without being asked twice.

## Relationship to the Party

Adversarial at first — the party had to strong-arm him into returning Snax's shield. Warmed up over several sessions. By Session 09, the party had fully won him over; he shared his backstory, helped decode the cipher, and supplied them with invisibility potions. Introduced them to Lok.

In Session 14 his secret was revealed: he fought in dragon form during the Battle of Saltmarsh, was knocked unconscious, and reverted to his true form — a silver dragon.

In Session 15, after recovering, he shifted back to halfling form and spoke openly with the party for the first time about what he is. He confirmed he is a silver dragon cursed by a necromancer, named the curse as the source of the black infection on his wing, and directly asked the party for their help in breaking it.

## Useful To The Party

- Supplies potions, healing kits, and magical goods (negotiated bulk deal: 200gp upfront + 200gp on delivery for 500gp worth of supplies).
- Decoded the Scarlet Brotherhood's ciphered letter.
- Introduced Lok as a companion.
- Had a run-in with Xindrad approximately 15 years ago — has personal knowledge of the dragon.
- In true form: a powerful combat asset.

## Story Notes

- The necromancer from Winston's past has moved from vague hint to active hook: he confirmed in Session 15 that a necromancer cursed him, that the black infection on his wing is the mark of that curse, and that he wants the party's help breaking it.
- The necromancer's identity and location are unknown.
- He has been living as a halfling shopkeeper in Saltmarsh, apparently in hiding or retirement from his true nature.
- His adventuring past and his confrontation with Xindrad suggest he was active in this region roughly 15 years ago.
