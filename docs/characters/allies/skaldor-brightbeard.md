# Skaldor Brightbeard

- **Role:** Ship captain; dwarven merchant
- **Race:** Dwarf
- **Affiliation:** Brightbeard Brothers trading company; captain of the Stonewind
- **Location:** Saltmarsh docks / Azure Sea
- **Note:** Introduced as "Skaldor Stonehelm" in Session 08; name revised to Brightbeard in Sessions 13–14.

## Appearance

Description unknown. The younger of the two Brightbeard brothers.

## Personality & Demeanor

Adventurous and enthusiastic — the more action-oriented counterpart to his pragmatic older brother Bramli. Jumped at the chance to fight in the Battle of Saltmarsh.

## Relationship to the Party

Captains the Stonewind, the brothers' ship. Transported the party to the Empress of the Waves (Session 08) and fought alongside them in the Battle of Saltmarsh (Session 14).

## Story Notes

- Confirmed enthusiastic participant in the Battle of Saltmarsh.
- Both brothers are from Granitepeak, a mountain region known for metalcraft, who left seeking adventure and trade before settling in Saltmarsh.
