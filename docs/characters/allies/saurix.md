# Saurix

- **Role:** Elder lizardfolk; overseer of the seven trials
- **Race:** Lizardfolk
- **Affiliation:** Lizardfolk Mound; has a history in Saltmarsh
- **Location:** Lizardfolk Mound

## Appearance

Elderly — one of the oldest lizardfolk in the mound.

## Personality & Demeanor

Deliberate and formal. Administered the Trial of Knowledge (riddles) personally and guided the party through the trial structure. Not hostile, but thorough — he holds the standards high and expects the party to earn the alliance.

## Relationship to the Party

Appointed by the Lizardfolk Queen to oversee the party's seven trials. Led them through Trials of Knowledge, Bravery, and Faith (Sessions 05–06). Neutral toward the party in a formal sense — he is evaluating, not befriending.

## Useful To The Party

- Living cultural bridge between Saltmarsh and the lizardfolk — he once lived in Saltmarsh.
- Institutional knowledge of both communities.

## Story Notes

- The fact that Saurix once lived in Saltmarsh is unusual and suggests a history between the two communities worth exploring.
- The shamans actively tried to sabotage the Trial of Faith (giant lizard wrangling) — Saurix's role in that interference, if any, was not addressed.
