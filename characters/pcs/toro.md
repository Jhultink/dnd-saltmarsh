## Personality

Curious and scholarly — took personal downtime to identify magical items and research obscure deities.

## Backstory

Arrived in Saltmarsh aboard the Sharkfin. Accepted the haunted house job for 100gp. Acquired the Staff of Fish Command (also called Trident of Fish Command) off a child in the Wicker Goat. Was identified as a spellcaster and carried the party's recovered spellbook.

## Abilities & Notable Skills

- Spellcaster with access to at least Rope Trick and Web.
- Identified the Luck Stone and researched the god Iuz at the Saltmarsh library.
- Used Rope Trick to rescue the party from the capsizing Empress of the Waves.
