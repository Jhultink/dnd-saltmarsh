Composed under pressure — stayed awake by Toro's grave the night the assassins attacked. Known to take initiative in negotiations and combat alike.

## Backstory

Arrived in Saltmarsh aboard the Sharkfin. The ship wrecked in a storm; Snax lost his shield to the sea but recovered it after strong-arming Winston. Took the haunted house job from Anders for 1 potion + 90gp.

## Abilities & Notable Skills

Skilled in unarmed combat — won the lizardfolk Trial of Strength in a 1v1 fight. Capable of crafting healing potions (found an alternate recipe for 20gp each; later learned the Greater Healing Potion recipe: 4d4+2, 100gp to craft). Delivered the killing blow on Xindrad the Fierce.
