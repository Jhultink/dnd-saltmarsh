# Sanbalet

- **Role:** Sea Princes arcane operative; assassin
- **Alias:** "Nunya"
- **Race:** 
- **Affiliation:** Sea Princes
- **Status:** Deceased — killed in the cottage attack (Session 12)

## Appearance

Description unknown. Escaped via rowboat in Session 02, making a quick exit under pressure.

## Personality & Motivation

A professional. Operated as the arcane contact for the haunted house smuggling front, then resurfaced as an assassin when the party threatened Sea Princes operations. His alias "Nunya" (as in "Nunya business") reflects his manner.

## Methods & Resources

- Arcane user — had a spellbook, signal codes, and a naval almanac at the haunted house base.
- Coordinated with the hooded figure and half-genasi fire woman (also Sea Princes agents) for the cottage assassination attempt.
- All three assassins bore Sea Princes tattoos.

## Relationship to the Party

First encountered (barely) in Session 02 — escaped by rowboat while Short Stacks was unconscious and the party couldn't pursue. Became a recurring loose thread. Finally caught and killed in Session 12 when he led the three-person assassination team to the party's cottage.

## Story Notes

- His escape in Session 02 was the cliffhanger that opened the Sea Ghost arc — the party used his signal codes and lantern to lure the Sea Ghost.
- His spellbook was recovered from the haunted house.
- The loose end of "Nunya escaped" was closed in Session 12 — a satisfying callback to the campaign's opening sessions.
