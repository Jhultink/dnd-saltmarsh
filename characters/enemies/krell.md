# Krell

- **Role:** Lolth cultist; occupant of the Empress of the Waves
- **Race:** Half-Orc
- **Affiliation:** Lolth-worshipping druid faction (one of two warring factions aboard the Empress)
- **Status:** Deceased — killed by the party (Session 08)

## Appearance

Half-orc. Found sitting at the center of a ceremonial circle in the hold of the Empress of the Waves.

## Personality & Motivation

Devout follower of Lolth, the Queen of Spiders. Demanded a blood sacrifice from the party when they entered his space. Unwilling to negotiate or back down.

## Methods & Resources

- Commanded two phase spiders.
- The ceremonial circle suggests ritual capability.

## Relationship to the Party

Encountered in the hold of the Empress of the Waves (Session 08). Demanded blood. The party refused. Combat ensued; Krell and both phase spiders were killed.

## Story Notes

- Krell represents one of two Lolth-worshipping factions that had taken over the Empress. Notes found aboard described the factions warring over the ship after the original crew was killed.
- The Empress of the Waves had an encounter with Lolth-worshipping island druids in a massive storm — the party found four captain's log entries documenting the crew's destruction.
- Krell's faction and the other Lolth faction's conflict was ongoing at the time of the party's visit.
