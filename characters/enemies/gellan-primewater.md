# Gellan Primewater

- **Role:** Town Councilmember (Traditionalist faction); Sea Princes smuggler
- **Race:** 
- **Affiliation:** Saltmarsh Town Council (public); Sea Princes (true allegiance)
- **Status:** Fled — disappeared from Saltmarsh between Sessions 11 and 12

## Appearance

Description unknown. Owns an estate in Saltmarsh with a basement full of weapon caches.

## Personality & Motivation

Presents as a cautious, non-confrontational council member — voted "do nothing" on both the smuggling and lizardfolk issues. In reality he is actively destabilizing Saltmarsh on behalf of the Sea Princes by arming both lizardfolk and Sahuagin simultaneously — deliberately engineering conflict on multiple fronts.

## Methods & Resources

- Weapon stash in his basement, labeled for delivery to both lizardfolk and Sahuagin.
- A network of bribes: paid local dockworkers, guards, and minor council officials to prevent dock inspections.
- Associated with a hooded figure and a half-genasi fire woman (later revealed as Sea Princes assassins).
- Was warned by an unsigned note to tighten security on "special shipments" after the party arrived in Saltmarsh.
- Has sabotaged competing merchants' ships.

## Relationship to the Party

The Stonehelm/Brightbeard brothers suspected Gellan of smuggling as early as Session 09. The party attempted to infiltrate his estate (Session 10); he threw them out. Short Stacks slipped in through the kitchen and confirmed the weapon caches (Session 11). The stolen notes Short Stacks took from his basement became key evidence against him.

## Story Notes

- Gellan vanished the night of the Sahuagin raid on Saltmarsh (Sessions 11–12). His whereabouts are unknown.
- His weapon stash was confiscated by Eda and repurposed for town defenses.
- The hooded figure and half-genasi fire woman from his basement showed up as assassins at the party's cottage in Session 12 — both were killed.
- Whether Gellan is still working for the Sea Princes from elsewhere, or has been eliminated as a loose end, is unknown.
