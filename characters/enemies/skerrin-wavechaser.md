# Skerrin Wavechaser

- **Role:** Anders Solmor's butler; Scarlet Brotherhood operative; council member (briefly)
- **Race:** 
- **Affiliation:** Scarlet Brotherhood; Anders Solmor
- **Status:** Deceased — killed in the Battle of Saltmarsh (Session 14)

## Appearance

Description unknown. Composed and professional in butler's role.

## Personality & Motivation

Operated entirely in the shadows within Anders' household. Flipped Anders' council vote against the party's Sea Ghost mission. Murdered Eliander Fireborn and framed the lizardfolk. Elevated to the council after that murder — likely by design, placing a Brotherhood operative in an official seat of power.

## Methods & Resources

- Exploited proximity to Anders and his mansion to gather intelligence and act against the party.
- Picked the lock of Eliander's back door and planted a lizardfolk sword to frame the murder.
- Spotted the party sneaking out of Anders' house with the cipher key — confirming he knew they were onto him.
- Fought as an open combatant on Anders' side during the Battle of Saltmarsh.

## Relationship to the Party

The party suspected him early — Manistrad voiced distrust of Skerrin (and Anders) in Session 07. They found the cipher key in his bedroom (Session 09). He clocked them on the way out. He fought them in Session 14 and was killed.

## Story Notes

- Murdered Eliander Fireborn and framed the lizardfolk — the murder that nearly derailed the lizardfolk alliance.
- Was appointed to replace Eliander on the council almost immediately — suspicious speed noted by Manistrad.
- Dead and unable to confess. Anders may be the only one who knows whether Skerrin killed Eliander on his orders or acted independently.
- A dark shape seen moving near Anders' mansion at 4am in Session 04 was likely Skerrin.
