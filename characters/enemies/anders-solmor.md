# Anders Solmor

- **Role:** Town Councilmember (Traditionalist faction); Scarlet Brotherhood agent
- **Race:** Human (appears young)
- **Affiliation:** Saltmarsh Town Council (public); Scarlet Brotherhood (true allegiance)
- **Status:** In custody — captured at end of Battle of Saltmarsh (Session 14)

## Appearance

Young, well-dressed council member. Owns a mansion in Saltmarsh with a butler (Skerrin).

## Personality & Motivation

Anders presents as a community-minded young official, but the persona is a cover. He hired the party to retrieve promissory notes from the Empress of the Waves — notes that turned out to relate to his Scarlet Brotherhood dealings. His public vote on council matters was influenced by Skerrin (notably voting against the party's mission on the Sea Ghost, flipped by Skerrin from his initial position).

What he wants: the Scarlet Brotherhood's goal is control of Saltmarsh. Anders has been their man on the inside.

## Methods & Resources

- Used Skerrin Wavechaser as a butler and operative within his household.
- Had contacts with the Sea Princes (promissory notes, the Empress of the Waves job).
- Revealed himself at the docks in Session 14 and called in a blue dragon — suggesting he has high-level supernatural backing.

## Relationship to the Party

Hired the party in Session 01 for the haunted house investigation — their first job. Appeared to be a patron and employer through Sessions 01–08. The party became suspicious of him via Skerrin's behavior and the ciphered letter. By Session 14, he dropped the pretense entirely, thanked them for recovering the promissory notes, and called in the blue dragon.

## Story Notes

- A ciphered letter found in his house (Session 08) contains: *"...we shall ensure that the Scarlet Brotherhood gains control of Saltmarsh... Act quickly and discreetly..."*
- Skerrin was his operative — Skerrin murdered Eliander Fireborn and framed the lizardfolk. Anders likely knew or ordered this.
- He is now in custody. He may know: who the blue dragon serves, whether he ordered Eliander's murder, and the full extent of Scarlet Brotherhood operations in Saltmarsh.
- The promissory notes he had the party retrieve from the Empress — their exact significance was never shared with the party.
