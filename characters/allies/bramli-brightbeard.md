# Bramli Brightbeard

- **Role:** Dwarven merchant; ship owner
- **Race:** Dwarf
- **Affiliation:** Brightbeard Brothers trading company; the Stonewind
- **Location:** Saltmarsh docks
- **Note:** Introduced as "Bramli Stonehelm" in Session 08; name revised to Brightbeard in Sessions 13–14.

## Appearance

Description unknown. The older of the two Brightbeard brothers.

## Personality & Demeanor

Pragmatic businessman — focused on commerce and practical outcomes. The more grounded counterpart to his adventurous younger brother Skaldor. Voiced suspicions about Gellan Primewater being a smuggler on the return voyage from the Empress of the Waves.

## Relationship to the Party

The party hired the Brightbeard brothers' ship, the Stonewind, to reach the derelict Empress of the Waves (Session 08). The brothers rowed the party over to the Empress then stood off on the Stonewind, picking the party up afterward. Fought Sahuagin alongside the party on the Stonewind during the Battle of Saltmarsh (Session 14).

## Story Notes

- Confirmed participant in the Battle of Saltmarsh — responded enthusiastically to Sharky's summons: "Hell yeah, always down for a fight!"
- Both brothers left the mountain region of Granitepeak (known for metalcraft) seeking adventure and trade, eventually settling in Saltmarsh as merchants.
