# Solomon

- **Role:** Garrison Captain of Saltmarsh
- **Race:** 
- **Affiliation:** Saltmarsh militia / town guard
- **Location:** Saltmarsh

## Appearance

Description unknown.

## Personality & Demeanor

Professional soldier. Keeps the town's defenses running. Tends to act on available intelligence even when that intelligence is wrong — was drilling soldiers and treating the party as dead after Oceanus' premature report.

## Relationship to the Party

Short Stacks made initial contact with Solomon while carousing at the Wicker Goat during downtime. Solomon fought alongside the party and their allies during the Battle of Saltmarsh (Session 14).

## Useful To The Party

- Coordinates town militia and guard deployments.
- A known quantity on the right side of the Saltmarsh political struggle.
- Can mobilize soldiers and manage town defenses during crisis.

## Story Notes

- When Oceanus returned to Saltmarsh claiming the party was dead (Session 07), Solomon began mobilizing forces and drilling soldiers based on that report — showing both competence and the risk of bad intel reaching him.
- Fought in the Battle of Saltmarsh alongside Rex, Captain Hex's pirates, lizardfolk warriors, Bullywugs, and Locathah.
