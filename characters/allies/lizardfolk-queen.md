# The Lizardfolk Queen

- **Role:** Ruler of the lizardfolk mound
- **Race:** Lizardfolk
- **Affiliation:** Lizardfolk Mound (approximately 10 miles west of Saltmarsh)
- **Location:** The Lizardfolk Mound

## Appearance

Description unknown — commands immediate authority in the mound.

## Personality & Demeanor

Intelligent and politically astute. Immediately saw through the party's lie that they worked for the Sea Princes — one of her own warriors from the Sea Ghost identified them. Rather than punishing the deception, she redirected to what mattered: determining whether the party was worth allying with. The seven-trial structure reflects a culture that values earned trust over stated intentions.

## Relationship to the Party

Caught the party attempting to bluff their way into the mound and put them through seven trials instead. After the party completed all seven — including killing Xindrad the Fierce — she honored the results. Sent the party back to Saltmarsh with a sealed letter for the council and provided 20 armed lizardfolk warriors as escorts and reinforcements.

## Useful To The Party

- Formal military alliance with the lizardfolk — 20 warriors who camped on the party's property and fought in the Battle of Saltmarsh.
- Political weight: the council's relationship with the lizardfolk is now the party's achievement.

## Story Notes

- The lizardfolk were buying weapons (via the Sea Ghost) to retake their homeland from the Sahuagin who seized it roughly a year ago — not to attack Saltmarsh.
- Not all lizardfolk support the Saltmarsh alliance — the shamans actively sabotaged the Trial of Faith.
- The Queen sent the party back with a sealed letter to the council; its contents were not detailed in the sessions.
- Xindrad the Fierce had been demanding monthly egg tributes (and escalating demands for gold and artifacts) — his death freed the mound from that burden.
