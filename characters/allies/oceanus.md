# Oceanus

- **Role:** Sea elf scout; guide
- **Race:** Sea Elf
- **Affiliation:** Tribe Manaan (undersea settlement ~20 miles SE of Saltmarsh)
- **Location:** Azure Sea / Saltmarsh region

## Appearance

Tall, even for a sea elf.

## Personality & Demeanor

Dutiful — was sent by his chief on a surveillance mission and was captured trying to follow his orders. Willing to work with surface-dwellers for a shared purpose.

## Relationship to the Party

Found imprisoned in a hidden cell aboard the Sea Ghost (Session 04). The party freed him. He explained his tribe and mission, and was hired by the council as a guide for 100gp (deducted from the council's 700gp payment). He later returned to Saltmarsh early claiming the party was dead — an error that caused unnecessary alarm.

## Useful To The Party

- Guide through the Azure Sea and surrounding waters.
- Direct connection to Tribe Manaan and the sea elf community.
- Was reached (no response) when Snax sent Sharky to rally allies before the Battle of Saltmarsh — his current status is unclear.

## Story Notes

- Oceanus' chief sent him to surveil the Sea Ghost. He witnessed weapons being loaded at its home port, was discovered, and spent an unspecified time as a prisoner.
- His premature report that the party was dead caused Solomon to mobilize soldiers unnecessarily in Session 07 — an honest mistake, not malice.
- Whether he fought in the Battle of Saltmarsh is unclear; he did not respond to Sharky's summons (Session 14).
