# Nalu

- **Role:** Locathah leader
- **Race:** Locathah
- **Affiliation:** Locathah tribe (lizardfolk allies)
- **Location:** Azure Sea / Saltmarsh coastal waters

## Appearance

Description unknown.

## Personality & Demeanor

Respectful and willing to honor a call for help. Responded promptly and graciously to Sharky's summons before the Battle of Saltmarsh.

## Relationship to the Party

The party met the Locathah during the Trial of Etiquette (Session 07) — won them over quickly through gifts, artistic expression, and cooking. The Locathah gave the party seaweed that extends breath-holding duration in return. Nalu responded to Sharky's battle summons with "We graciously accept your request" (Session 14).

## Story Notes

- The Locathah suffered significant losses during the Battle of Saltmarsh — Locathah bodies were found throughout the harbor area (Session 14), meaning they bore heavy casualties defending the docks.
- They are allies of the lizardfolk mound, not of Saltmarsh directly — the party's relationship is through the lizardfolk alliance.
